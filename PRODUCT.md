# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Individuals tracking personal spending. They import bank CSVs or PDFs, review where money goes, set category budgets, and occasionally share a spending summary with a partner or roommate. Context: checking the app on a phone during downtime or on a laptop after a bank statement drops. Not finance professionals; people who want clarity without complexity.

## Product Purpose

Stash replaces spreadsheet-based expense tracking with a faster, more visual workflow. Import a statement, see categorized breakdowns instantly, spot anomalies, set budgets. Success: users understand their spending patterns in under 30 seconds after uploading a statement.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, clear, helpful. Like a smart friend who's good with money. Confident but never condescending. Celebrates your wins (under budget!) without guilt-tripping overspending. Uses plain language, not financial jargon.

## Anti-references

- NOT traditional finance UI: no navy-and-gold, no serif fonts, no stock imagery of handshakes or skylines. This should never feel like a bank portal.
- NOT Bloomberg/trading density: no overwhelming data grids or neon-on-black chart walls.
- NOT generic fintech (Mint-style): not the default teal-and-white, rounded-everything, illustration-heavy budget app that blends in with every competitor.
- Embrace a bit of playfulness. Finance doesn't have to be serious all the time.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over density.** Every screen answers one question. If it takes more than a glance, simplify.
2. **Warmth in precision.** Numbers are exact (monospace, tabular); everything around them is human and approachable.
3. **Celebrate the data.** Charts and breakdowns are the star, not decoration. Make the data feel rewarding to look at.
4. **Invisible complexity.** Auto-categorization, duplicate detection, recurring transactions: powerful features that don't demand attention.
5. **Your vault, your rules.** Private by default. Sharing is opt-in and explicit.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratios. Full keyboard navigation. Screen reader support with ARIA landmarks and labels. Reduced motion support via `prefers-reduced-motion`.
